RAFA Academy — Powered by Get Innn Sports

Elite Football Development

This Is Where Your
Football Journey Begins

We don't just train players — we build competitors. Structured, science-backed football development for every level and every age.

01⚽

Foundation Programme

Ages 6–10. Building fundamental football skills, coordination, and love for the game through structured play-based learning.

02🎯

Development Programme

Ages 11–14. Technical skill development, positional understanding, and tactical awareness with progressive training loads.

03🏆

Elite Performance

Ages 15–19. High-intensity training for players targeting professional pathways. Competition-focused development throughout.

04🧤

Goalkeeper Academy

Specialist goalkeeper training covering shot-stopping, distribution, commanding the area, and modern GK techniques.

05🧠

Mental Performance

Sports psychology sessions to build focus, resilience, and competitive mindset — the mental edge that separates good from great.

06🌍

International Exposure

Through our TalentX UK partnership, selected players get exposure to international training standards and professional environments.
